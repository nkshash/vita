About This Bot

Welcome to the About page! At VITA (Virtual Interactive Treatment Advisor), we're committed to revolutionizing healthcare accessibility, especially for rural and underserved communities. Our AI-powered healthcare chatbot is designed to break down barriers to quality healthcare by leveraging cutting-edge technology, including image recognition and natural language processing. With a vast database of medical knowledge and continuous learning capabilities, our chatbot provides personalized and actionable advice, helping users manage their health effectively. We're passionate about empowering individuals to take control of their well-being, no matter where they are.

Diseases Covered:

Gingivitis

Gum Disease. Occurs when plaque and bacteria build up on your teeth and cause infection.

Mouth Ulcers

Painful sores on inner lips, gums, tongue, roof of the mouth or throat, such as a canker sore.

Tooth Discolouration

The staining or darkening of teeth.

Abrasions

An area damaged by scraping or wearing away.

Bruises

An injury appearing as an area of discoloured skin caused by a blow or impact rupturing blood vessels.

Burns

An injury caused by exposure to heat or flame (first and second degrees only).

Cuts/Lacerations

A deep cut or tear in skin or flesh.

Diabetic Wounds

A serious complication caused by poor circulation, susceptibility to infection, and nerve damage from high blood sugar levels.

Surgical Wounds

A cut through the skin made during surgery.

Venous Wounds

A wound on the leg or ankle caused by abnormal or damaged veins.

Cellulitis

A skin condition causing lumpy, dimpled flesh on the thighs, hips, buttocks, and abdomen.

Impetigo

A contagious bacterial skin infection forming pustules and yellow crusty sores.

Athlete’s Foot

A fungal infection that usually begins between the toes.

Nail Fungus

A common condition that begins as a white or yellow spot under the tip of your fingernail or toenail.

Psoriasis

A skin disease that causes red, itchy scaly patches, most commonly on the knees, elbows, trunk, and scalp.
