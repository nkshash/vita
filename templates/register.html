VITA Terms and Conditions

Introduction

Welcome to VITA, an AI-powered healthcare chatbot application designed to assist users with medical advice and information. By accessing and using our services, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following Terms and Conditions, which incorporate both our Data Handling Policy and Image Handling Policy. These policies are designed to safeguard your personal and medical data, ensuring that we handle it responsibly and securely, in compliance with applicable laws and regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information:

  • Contact details: such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Medical information: including medical conditions, medical history, allergies, lifestyle, and demographic information like age, gender, and race.

2. Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP addresses: for location tracking and service optimization.
  • Usage data: such as log data, browsing history, and interaction patterns with the chatbot.

3. Images Provided by Users:

Users may upload images of medical conditions for diagnosis or advice, which may contain personal and sensitive information.

Password Security:

User passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption protocols and securely stored, ensuring protection against unauthorized access.

How We Use Your Information and Images

1. Provision of Services:

  • To provide timely and personalized medical advice and support.
  • To analyze images using recognition algorithms to identify features indicative of medical conditions.
  • To allow IBM services, integrated into our application, to provide secure cloud storage, data processing, and system performance optimization.

2. Improvement of Services:

  • To analyze user interactions and feedback to improve chatbot performance and accuracy.
  • To enhance our image recognition algorithms and overall service offerings through machine learning.
  • To enhance our image recognition algorithms and overall service offerings using machine learning, while recognizing that AI models, including ours, can be fallible. The current accuracy of our model is 86.22%, meaning there is a possibility of incorrect or incomplete recommendations.

3. Compliance and Protection:

  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure the integrity and security of your data.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements.

Data and Image Retention

Medical Information: Retained for the duration of your interaction with the chatbot, plus any period required by healthcare regulations.

Personal Information: Retained while you maintain an account with us and for a reasonable period thereafter to meet legal obligations.

Usage Data: Typically retained for up to 24 months for analysis and service improvement.

Medical Images: Retained while interacting with the chatbot; anonymized images may be retained longer for research and service improvement.

Data and Image Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information and images. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Both in transit and at rest using protocols like AES-256 and TLS (Transport Layer Security).
  • Access Controls: Access to personal and image data is restricted through role-based access controls (RBAC) with multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • Audit Logs: All access and modifications to personal data and images are logged with details such as timestamps and user IDs, with regular audits for compliance.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the minimum necessary data to provide our services and anonymize or pseudonymous images where possible.
  • Backup and Disaster Recovery: Data and images are backed up regularly in encrypted, geographically distributed locations to ensure availability in case of a disaster.

User Rights

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal data or images through the app or by contacting us directly.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data or images at any time. Requests will be processed in line with applicable laws and our retention policies.
  • Revocation of Consent: You may revoke your consent for the processing of your personal data or images by contacting us. Upon revocation, we will cease processing your data, except where required by law.

IBM Services and Third-Party Access

Our app leverages IBM cloud services for secure data storage, processing, and backup. As part of this integration, IBM may have access to the information provided by users during the use of our app. While IBM adheres to stringent privacy standards, by using our services, you acknowledge this potential third-party access to your data.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information or images from children under 13. If we discover that such data has been inadvertently collected, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

No Log Sharing

In the event of any malfunction or breach, no audit logs or detailed system logs will be shared with third parties to ensure your privacy. All internal access to logs and data will remain strictly controlled within the limits outlined in our Data Handling Policy.
